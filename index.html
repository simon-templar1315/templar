wild animals

A wild animal is an animal that is not domesticated and lives in the wild. They are not used to being around people and can be dangerous if they feel threatened. Wild animals can be found all over the world, in a variety of habitats, including forests, deserts, oceans, and mountains.

    domestic animals

    Domestic animals are animals that have been bred by humans over generations to live in close association with people. They are often used for food, transportation, companionship, or labor. Domestic animals have been a part of human society for thousands of years, and they play an important role in our lives.
